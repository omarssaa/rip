The pH is the measure of the concentration of positive hydrogen ions [H+](protons for short) or hydronium ions [H3O+] in a solution. It is also the measure of the acidity or alkalinity(basicity) of a solution.

The pH is based on a logarithmic scale ranging from 0 as a minmum value to 14 as a maximum value.

\(pH=-log([H^+])\)

or

\(pH=-log([H_3 O^+])\)

Where the square brackets refer to the concentration of the protons or hydronium ions.
